About SWEET HILL Saint-Barthelemy

HAVEN OF PEACE AND REFINEMENT

The island of Saint-Barthélémy has become the destination par excellence for visitors from all over the world, not only for the enchanting calm of the island, but also for the beauty of its landscapes and its beaches.

Created in 2021 , Sweet Hill is a property bringing together several new, comfortable and tastefully decorated accommodations.

Perched in the mountains of Petite Saline, Sweet Hill offers very comfortable accommodation. In this place, you will take full advantage of a calm environment and beautiful panoramas.
